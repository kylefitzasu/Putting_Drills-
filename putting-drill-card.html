Core Drills
01
Gate Drill
Perfect Putt Fixes: Push Right
Set two tees just wider than your putter face, 6 inches ahead of the ball. Roll putts through the gate from 5 feet. The gate eliminates face manipulation — you physically cannot steer through it.
YT "putting gate drill" Dave Pelz
10 REPS · DAILY
"Path, not aim"
02
Headcover Drill
Any Hole Fixes: Arms Crossing
Tuck a headcover under each armpit before stroking. If either drops, arms are working independently. Forces the connected, shoulder-driven stroke. Core rewiring drill for your arm-swing habit.
YT "headcover under armpits putting drill"
20 REPS · DAILY
"Arms are rods, not levers"
03
Rhythm Count
Gentleman's Fixes: Decel / Yips
Count "one" on backstroke, "two" at impact. Focus only on the count — not the result. Builds automaticity. A metronomic stroke holds up under pressure; a silent, conscious stroke does not.
YT "putting tempo drill one two" Adam Young Golf
10 REPS · DAILY
"One… two. Always two."
04
Made Putt Streak
Regular Hole Builds Confidence
Make 5 in a row from 4–5 feet. Miss one, start over. Always end every session this way — never end on a miss. Confidence memory is real. Your brain remembers the last thing that happened.
YT "5 in a row putting drill pressure"
5-STREAK · END OF SESSION
"End on success, always"
05
Clock Drill
Pinery Green Fixes: 10-Foot Pull Exposes Dominant Miss
SETUP
Place 6 balls around the hole at 5 feet — 12, 2, 4, 6, 8, and 10 o'clock positions. Must make all 6 before moving on.
WHAT TO WATCH
Misses cluster at specific positions. Left-side misses (8–10 o'clock) = shoulder rotation pulling path. Right-side = hands steering face.
THE FIX
When you find your miss cluster, pause and reset — seesaw cue for pulls, gate cue for pushes. Don't grind; diagnose and adjust.
YT "clock drill putting 6 balls" Dave Stockton
6-BALL COMPLETE ROUND · PINERY ONLY
"Find it, fix it, move on"
06
Alignment Stick Drill
Pinery Green Fixes: 10-Foot Pull Grooves: Seesaw Rock
Lay a stick on the ground parallel to your target line, just outside your feet. Make 10-foot putts keeping your shoulder rock parallel to the stick. The moment your trail shoulder crosses it, you've rotated instead of rocked. Your pull disappears when the stick stays clean.
YT "alignment stick putting drill shoulder rotation"
10 REPS · PINERY SESSIONS
"Seesaw, not revolving door"
